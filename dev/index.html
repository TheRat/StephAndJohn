Allow us to reintroduce ourselves

You'll probably know one or both of us, but this might be a good opportunity for us to fill you in a little on how we got here. I'm sure our story will come up a few times on the 20th of October, but here's some bullet points to get you up to speed.

Our first date was at 'The Stand' in Glasgow in 2011 where we took in a bit of comedy. As with any good love story we decided to remain friends at first, and continued to text each other and meet up every so often over the next 5 years.

On Valentines day in 2016 we both realised that there might be something more to be had with our platonic relationship, and decided to start seeing if there could be something there.

Just 7 short months later things seemed to be working out, so much so that we were engaged on the 28th of September 2016 in the city of Florence, Italy.

At the start of this year we moved in together and have been busy making our new house a home together. We're both excited to be continuing our adventure together with our marriage in October

Getting There

Car

There is plenty of car parking available at the venue should you wish to drive

Train

The nearest stop is 'Dumbreck' - one stop from Glasgow Central on the 'Paisley Canal' line.

Trains are every 30 minutes, with the final train to Glasgow leaving at 11:18pm

Taxi

From the central of Glasgow a 'Black Hack' will cost you about £10 to/from the venue.

Wedding Coach

A coach will be travelling from Ayr and Kilmarnock at around 5:30pm for the evening guests.

The coach will reverse this route at 1am, dropping off in Kilmarnock and Ayr. Details will be circulated nearer the time.
